The Blue Plantation

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz white rum
  • 1 oz blue curaçao
  • 1 oz pineapple juice
  • 1 oz coconut cream
  • 1/2 oz lime juice
  • Ice
  • Pineapple slice and maraschino cherry (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. In a cocktail shaker, combine the white rum, blue curaçao, pineapple juice, coconut cream, and lime juice.
  2. Add ice to the shaker and shake well until the mixture is chilled.
  3. Strain the mixture into a chilled glass filled with ice.
  4. Garnish with a pineapple slice and a maraschino cherry.
  5. Serve and enjoy!

Dietary Information:

Diet Conscious Measurements:

Lower Alcohol Option:

  • 1 oz white rum (instead of 2 oz)
  • 0.5 oz blue curaçao (instead of 1 oz)
  • Extra pineapple juice to taste

Lower Sugar Option:

  • Sugar-free blue syrup (instead of blue curaçao)
  • Light coconut milk (instead of cream)
  • Unsweetened pineapple juice
