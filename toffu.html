Dietary Adaptations:

For Gluten-Free:

  • Use tamari instead of soy sauce.

For Low-Sodium:

  • Reduce soy sauce and use coconut aminos.
  • Use less sesame oil or omit it.

For Oil-Free:

  • Omit sesame oil in dressing and marinade.
  • Bake tofu without oil.

For Low-Sugar:

  • Skip honey/maple syrup or use stevia.

For High-Protein:

  • Increase tofu or add more edamame.
  • Sprinkle hemp seeds for extra protein.
